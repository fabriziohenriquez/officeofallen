About

Who is E. Allen Chandler?

A Retired Army Lieutenant Colonel, who ran the Army’s Criminal Law Division is also a successful and accomplished advocate with both extensive experience as a Prosecutor and Criminal Defense Attorney (Texas and California); a Military Law Expert (Claims, Torts, and the Manual for Courts- Martial); and Legal Veteran of the multiple military deployments such as Bosnia-Herzegovina and Croatia (Dayton Peace Accords); Iraq, Kuwait, and Saudi Arabia (Desert Shield/Desert Storm); and Korea, Germany, and Japan (Europe and Asia). Since leaving the service, Allen has been a California Attorney; Dallas County Prosecutor; and was President of a Legal Company providing lawyers to those accused of crimes in more than 20 states; before opening his law practice. He has more than 35 years of Military and Civilian Legal Experience in front of Courts, Boards, Judges, Juries, and Arbitration. He is a fighter and legal warrior who can fight for you.
